CompiKode

CompiKode is the ultimate competitive coding event where your coding skills and problem-solving abilities will be put to the test! Open to all years, this thrilling event features three action-packed rounds filled with real-world challenges. In Round 1, race through fast-paced problems, and the top team will get the power to choose between "Time Turns" (extra time) or "Clue Cracks" (valuable hints), with the second-place team receiving the remaining advantage. Round 2 amps up the complexity, giving the top teams a boost to tackle even tougher problems. And in Round 3, the stakes are higher than ever as the remaining competitors battle for the prestigious title of Ultimate Champion! Don’t miss out on this exciting opportunity to prove you’ve got what it takes to come out on top!
